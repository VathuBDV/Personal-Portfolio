Poker Game

This is a CLI Poker game where the user starts off with a bankroll of 1000$. At each round, the user is dealt 5 cards and he has the option to discard and replace up to 4 of the 5 cards. Based on the hand that the user has, the user will win a certain amount of money. The user has the option to play until he is out of money or to quit after a certain amount of rounds.

This was my final project for my Programming II course during my Winter semester of the year 2020.
